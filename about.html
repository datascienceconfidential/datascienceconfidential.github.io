Data Science Confidential

This is a blog about the theory and practice of statistics, machine learning, data visualisation, and so on. I have a background in academia and work as a data scientist in industry. Here's a link to my homepage with some other projects I have done, as well as lecture notes from old courses. I am also very interested in economics and other topics.

None of the information in this blog is confidential. The title was inspired by the movie Kansas City Confidential.
